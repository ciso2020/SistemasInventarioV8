You just need to exclude the obj folder from the project/solution. If you don't know how to do this, add this to your .csproj or .vbproj or .whateverproj:
{
---esto hacerlo
  <ItemGroup>
    <Compile Remove="obj\**" />
    <Content Remove="obj\**" />
    <EmbeddedResource Remove="obj\**" />
    <None Remove="obj\**" />
  </ItemGroup>
